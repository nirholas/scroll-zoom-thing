Overview

PAI is a bootable USB Linux distribution that runs Ollama and Open WebUI locally. A complete offline AI workstation that lives entirely in RAM and leaves zero trace on your host machine.

Debian 12 base, Sway desktop, hardened defaults. Works on any AMD64 or ARM64 hardware. No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry.

Quickstart

Download the ISO, flash a USB stick, and boot from it. First launch takes under two minutes from power-on to a working local AI chat.

  1. Grab the latest ISO from pai.direct
  2. Flash with dd, Rufus, or Raspberry Pi Imager
  3. Boot your machine from USB and pick a model
